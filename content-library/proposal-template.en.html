Restored to a sellable condition per {{COMPANY}} standards, with work items and packaging materials agreed in advance.

01 · Precision inspection spec Starts on inbound
  • Photo records — high-resolution 3-side photography, focused recording of major contamination/damage
  • Component check — consistency check for missing set components/free gifts
  • Packaging and damage judgment — opened-package damage analysis, real-time recording of pass/fail judgment in the system
Uploaded to the Returneeds portal immediately upon judgment
02 · Standard refurbishment work Core of value restoration
  • Roll/contact cleaning — precise removal of dust and hair
  • Loose-thread trimming — fixing poor finishing and protruding threads
  • Fine solid-stain wiping — precision cleaning of light stains such as cosmetics
  • {{COMPANY}} precision refolding — folding per garment-specific guide standards
  • Standard poly-bag repackaging — sealed packaging in dedicated clear poly bags
Goal: restore to sellable, normal inventory
03 · Add-on reconditioning options Applied after agreement
  • Tag/label replacement — removing damaged tags and reprinting genuine-grade tags
  • Tissue-paper/packaging-box replacement — replacing creased internal tissue paper, transferring out of damaged outer boxes
  • Fine de-wrinkling (steam) — garment-specific hanger-type high-pressure steam pressing when fitting wrinkles occur
Applied per agreed standards after packaging materials are transferred
